Common questions
Everything you need to know
How is this better than a school trip to London? ▾
Students speak far more English per day with LevelUp than on a typical trip abroad — where they spend most time together in their native language. Our programs are designed so English is the only option, all day, every day. And you save 40–60% on travel costs.
Who are the trainers? ▾
All trainers are native English speakers from the UK, US, Ireland, and beyond — with professional backgrounds in education, performance, or business. They're not just fluent; they know how to make language learning happen naturally.
Can schools use Startchancen funding? ▾
Yes. We're experienced in working with Startchancen-Förderung and can help you understand how to apply it to your program. Mention it when you inquire and we'll guide you through the process.
Where do programs take place? ▾
Across Germany — in youth hostels, nature camps, and guesthouses selected for safety and atmosphere. Some programs also run in Austria and Switzerland. We handle all venue logistics, so you don't have to.
How long does booking take? ▾
Reach out, get a custom quote within 48 hours, confirm when ready. We handle everything from there — venue, trainer scheduling, parent info packs, and pre-trip prep materials. Most programs book 4–8 weeks in advance.
